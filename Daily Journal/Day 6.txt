Project Journal: Day 6
The "Whack-a-Mole" Breakthrough

Days 4 & 5 Recap: I didn't journal because the gap detection was incredibly difficult. Fighting with bounding boxes and Tesseract OCR felt like hitting a wall, as the "blast radius" of digital shapes kept breaking the engine.

Today’s Milestone: Finally completed Phase 1: Damage Detection.

Technical Wins:

Heuristic Math: Built a script that measures pixel distances to guess the number of missing words.

Spellchecker Guardrails: Integrated pyspellchecker to kill OCR "hallucinations" (garbage text) while keeping real words safe.

Multilingual Support: Successfully processed both Modern English and 1,000-year-old Anglo-Saxon by loading a custom 42,000-word Old English dictionary from GitHub.

Status: Phase 1 is officially bilingual and almost stable.
