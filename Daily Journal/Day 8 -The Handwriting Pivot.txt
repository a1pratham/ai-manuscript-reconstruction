Day 8: The Handwriting Pivot

Tried feeding real cursive to Tesseract today and it completely bombed—just spat out a massive wall of gap tags. Turns out standard OCR can't handle connected letters at all.

Switched gears and brought in Microsoft's TrOCR AI. It actually read the messy handwriting perfectly! We tried building a complicated "hybrid" engine to combine both tools, but it was way too slow and clunky.

I made the call to just keep it simple: the app will just have a toggle for the user to choose "Printed" (runs Tesseract) or "Handwritten" (runs TrOCR). It keeps the model way lighter and faster. I dumped the hybrid code into an archive folder just so I don't lose the logic, but the extraction phase is officially done.

Ready for Phase 2 tomorrow!
